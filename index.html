Our Story

The Woven Landscape

Mala Hiša — “the little house” — is a Nordic-inspired retreat tucked into one of Slovenia's most beautiful glacial valleys. Here, high-end natural materials and honest craftsmanship meet the raw beauty of the Alps.

Every detail, from hand-woven linen to locally sourced timber, is chosen to bring the calm of the outdoors inside. Wake to birdsong, breathe mountain air, and let the valley set the pace.
