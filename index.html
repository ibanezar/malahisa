Our Story

A Piece of Fairy Tale

If you want to get away from your worries, avoid the crowds, or simply rest in a peaceful place — Mala Hiša sits in Robanov Kot, beneath the mountain Ojstrica, in a glacial valley almost untouched by tourism. Nowhere else will you find such harmony between natural and cultural landscape.

Robanov Kot is one of the three valleys of Solčavsko — a European Destination of Excellence and a textbook example of the Alpine valleys and their green meadows. Treat yourself and your loved ones to a stay in this natural pearl.

Experiences

Live the Valley

From sunrise hikes to the first winter snow, the days here are yours to shape.

Mountain Hiking

An excellent base for ascents of the surrounding peaks — the trail to the top of Ojstrica (2,343 m) rewards you with a spectacular view. Or take the gentle hour-long walk to the alpine dairy.

Cycling & Panoramic Route

Ride out to the famous Logar Valley and Matkov Kot, or follow the Solčava Panoramic Route that winds through the high farms of the valley.

Winter & Snow

Snowshoeing, sledging and crisp winter walks when the valley turns white.
